We Are Blob

"We Are Blob" is a cozy point-and-click game about Blob, a newly hired game developer racing to complete their first game in one week. It blends resource management with storytelling. Available on Steam.

  • Development Time: 6 months
  • Team Size: 25 people
  • Studio: Black Banshee Studios

Narrative Design

  • Wrote a branching storyline with multiple player-driven outcomes.
  • Created tutorial dialogues, in-game emails, and text messages.
  • Designed fun product names for "Whamazon" and creative titles for "iTube."
  • Authored prompts and help screens to guide players effectively.

Game Design

  • Drafted key documents, including GDD and TDD.
  • Balanced hunger, health, and stress systems for engaging gameplay.
  • Designed Blob's room layout for optimal playability.

Programming & Leadership

  • Led a team of 4 developers, maintaining high coding standards.
  • Developed laptop, phone systems, UI, and save/load functionality.
  • Implemented cutscene triggers and branching storyline systems.

Quality Assurance

  • Performed extensive QA testing and logged issues via JIRA.
  • Authored detailed QA documentation for efficient debugging.
